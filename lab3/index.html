Chapter 1: In the Beginning

In the beginning, John had never heard of programming. In fact, he did not even know what a computer was. His parents had an office computer and his interest in technology was sparked. But he had already set his mind on becoming a teacher just the same as his parents and grandma and grandpa. Should he become a computer teacher?




Chapter 2: What Now

John had a choice to make. He could combine both and become a teacher, or he could focus on one or another. Throughout high school, he debated this back and forth. Then, one day, his high school was hacked and valuable information was stolen. John knew what he wanted to be.




Chapter 3: We're Saved

He didn't want any school to be hacked again. So he decided he wanted to become a computer scientist and become a hero for everyone by saving every school from being hacked. In just his first year in college, he made an unhackable security system, and no school was ever hacked again.
